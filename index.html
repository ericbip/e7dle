How to Play

Objective

Guess the hidden Epic Seven hero of the day! A new hero is revealed every day.

How to Guess

  • Type a hero name in the search box
  • Select from the autocomplete dropdown
  • Each guess reveals colored tiles with feedback
  • You have unlimited guesses!

Tile Colors

✓
Green — Exact match! This attribute is correct.
✗
Grey — No match. This attribute differs.

Attributes

  • Element — Fire, Ice, Wind, Earth, Light, Dark
  • Role — Warrior, Knight, Thief, Mage, Ranger, Soul Weaver
  • Zodiac — The hero's star sign
  • Gender — Male or Female
  • Rarity — 3★, 4★, or 5★
